The Ripper

Are you ready to be on the edge of your seat for the whole trip? If so, then this trip is for you. This trip is made for more advanced rafters, and those seeking adventure. We launch the raft straight into some mild rapids that quickly pick up pace. The trip is in strong rapids and will be sure to provide an adventure. Snacks are provided and this trip lasts about 4 hours.

The Lounger

Every family will love this trip because it is desiged with them in mind. We take you down a calm river that will provide a safe intro to water rafting. Part way through the trip we reach a small patch of mild rapids that will provide some extra fun during the trip. Lunch is provided. This trip lasts about two hours and, if needed, can be ended early.

The Bay

This trip will be found no where else. On this trip you will enter rapids from almost the start. From then on we will have patches of rapids and calm areas, all culmonating in thr trip of a lifetime. The river ends in a wonderful, pristine lake that provides a good view of water life and the surrounding nature. This trip lasts about 4 hours and lunch is provided at the lake.
